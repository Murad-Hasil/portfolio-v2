## Service 4: RAG / Knowledge Base Systems

**Best for**: Companies wanting AI that answers accurately from their own data — product documentation, legal policies, support FAQs, internal knowledge bases.

### What You Get
Retrieval-Augmented Generation systems that give AI models the ability to answer questions from your specific company content — without hallucination. The AI retrieves the relevant sections, then generates a grounded, cited response.

### Deliverables
1. Vector database (Qdrant or pgvector) populated with your company's knowledge, properly chunked and embedded

3. Automated update pipeline so new documents are indexed automatically when you add them

### Example Work
